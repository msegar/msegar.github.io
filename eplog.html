A case log built for EP

Track every procedure, outcome, and complication with smart checklists designed around how electrophysiologists actually work.

Coming Soon to the App Store

Tracking cases in a spreadsheet that hasn't been updated since fellowship? Pulling together a case list for credentialing from memory? Logging procedures in a system that doesn't know the difference between an AF ablation and an EP study?

EPLog replaces ad hoc workflows with a purpose-built tool. Every procedure type has its own structured checklist. Every field reflects current EP practice. Your data stays on your device.

What EPLog Does

Everything you need to maintain a detailed, credentialing-ready case archive.

Smart Procedure Checklists

Dynamic forms for 10 procedure types with conditional fields. Select PFA energy and the system options update. Choose atypical flutter and the location picker appears. No irrelevant fields.

CHA₂DS₂-VASc Scoring

Auto-calculated from patient data, color-coded by risk, and snapshotted at each case. See the score at a glance on every patient and case card.

Image Capture

Attach images from your camera, photo library, or the built-in document scanner. Images land in an inbox so you can assign them to the right case later.

Outcome Tracking

Log arrhythmia recurrences with detection method—symptom, Holter, wearable monitor, or device interrogation—tied back to the patient and original case.

CSV Export

Generate a credentialing-ready spreadsheet with one tap. Filter by date range, and every procedure attribute is broken out into its own column.

Analytics Dashboard

Case volume trends over time, procedure breakdowns by type and category, and percentage distributions—all visualized with native iOS charts.

Built Around EP Workflows

Ten procedure types with fields that reflect current practice—CARTO, EnSite X, and Opal mapping systems; RF and PFA energy with specific catheter and system options; 16 complication categories.
